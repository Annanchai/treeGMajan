About Us

treeG

treeG group owns a reputed eye hospital in India with a proven track record of more than 500,000 patient strength and it is a matter of pride that we have conducted above 50,000 successful surgeries. We are now embarking on ambitious business projects in and outside India that offer abundant opportunities to grow thereby establishing a stable position in the global business arena with collaborative efforts.

Majan Eye Centre

Majan Eye Centre LLC group is an entity of young experienced Omani entrepreneurs with an immense experience in manifold industries. We have enormous accomplishments in construction, real estate, food industry and legal firms which we have attained from constant striving and hard work.

treeG Majan Eye Centre

Amalgamation of both the groups resulted in the evolution of treeG Majan Eye centre to serve the purpose of eradicating the requisite for an affordable eye treatment among the Omani population. Here, we endeavor to give vision to suffering millions and create avenues for enthusiastic medical professionals to grow with us.
